About Jakarta

  • Based on its experience providing legal advice and resolving disputes for Korean companies operating in Indonesia, Yoon & Yang opened its Korean Desk in November 2018 at Amir Syamsudin & Partners. Amir Syamsudin & Partners was established by Amir Syamsudiamir, an attorney who had served as the Minister of Law and Human Rights in Indonesia.

    Indonesia is abundant in natural resources with a massive population of 250 million. For the last several years, the country has shown steady economic growth. Korean companies are continuing to broaden their presence in industries such as forestry & resources development, textile, apparel & footwear toll processing, as well as proactively seeking to gain access into industries such as large-scale manufacturing industries (e.g., automobile, steel, petrochemical, energy), various service industries (e.g., finance and distribution), and other business opportunities related to commercial & residential real estate and infrastructure development. In addition, the Korean government has been focusing on Indonesia as a vital country to implement its New Southern Policy.

  • For the purpose of adhering to the needs of Korean companies and clients, Yoon & Yang’s Korean Desk in Indonesia is headed by Mr. Ji Hoon Cha (partner), who has multiple years of experience advising Korean companies operating in Indonesia in the areas of finance, M&A, real estate & construction, and international arbitration. Together with the close cooperation of more than 400 attorneys and employees at Yoon & Yang’s Head Office in Seoul, the Indonesia Korean Desk provides high-quality legal services in diverse fields ranging from M&A, finance, real estate & construction, energy, establishment & dissolution of local subsidiaries and arbitration & dispute resolution.

    Yoon & Yang’s Indonesia Korean Desk will further concentrate efforts to offer high quality legal services for clients tailored to local circumstances, based on accumulated experience and professional knowledge and up-to-date legal information through close cooperation with trustworthy and qualified local professionals.
