GHS
Globally Harmonized System of Classification and domestic of Chemicals
GHS is a system that easily and consistently delivers warning labels and safety rules for chemicals that differ by country through MSDS written according to internationally unified classification and labeling.
CLP
Classification, Labelling & Packaging
CLP is a common European scheme to achieve the goals of the GHS faster, easier and more definitely.
