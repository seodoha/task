MSDS authoring program(software) – “SDS_Pro”
MSDS is required to be prepared in the official language of the exporting country. The GHS standards vary from by country, and the legal data prepared for MSDS shall be prepared with the legal data of each of the countries. In the particular case of compounds, the logic of GHS classification is highly complex, and a great amount of time and costs are generated when preparing the MSDS.

SDS_Pro, NAM & NAM International Co., Ltd.’s MSDS automatic creation program, can write, computerize, and manage MSDS for mono and multi constituent substances in a quick but effective manner.
Characteristics of Program
  • 5 languages supported
    (Korean, English, Chinese, Japanese, Taiwanese)
  • One-click
    printing of labels
  • Item management
    through the admin page
  • Possible to set/change the classification of substances as per customer’s requirements
  • Preparation of simple mixture MSDS based on customer’s single substance information
  • Foreign language MSDS can be made by clicking ‘foreign language’ button
  • Can be customized according to user convenience through simple usage
  • Easy to maintain security by using an internal server with the server installation method
