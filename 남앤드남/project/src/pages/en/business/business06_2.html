MSDS Service
MSDS preparation according to GHS classification
It is obligatory for export to provide SDS written in accordance with the applicable language and regulations, and to provide MSDS in Korean when transferring substances in Korea.
‘Label’ preparation
It means writing a warning label in accordance with the Occupational Safety and Health Act and the Chemical Control Act, and it must be attached to the outer surface of the product container, etc.
MSDS reliability review
It refers to improving the reliability of the MSDS prepared by reviewing whether the latest regulations and regulatory-related laws have been applied.
MSDS submission
In accordance with the revised Occupational Safety and Health Act that took effect from 2020, substances or products subject to MSDS must submit it to the Ministry of Employment and Labor. A special period is given according to the tonnage range of the quantity previously handled for each product, and related regulations must be implemented within the period.
Application for Confidential Business Information(CBI) in MSDS.(as representative)
In order to apply trade secrets to the substance composition of item 3 of the MSDS under the Occupational Safety and Health Act, which comes into effect from 2021, an application must be made to the Ministry of Employment and Labor.
