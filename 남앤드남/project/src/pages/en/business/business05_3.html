Notice of small volume of non-Phase-in substances
  • Notice the total volume of national environmental emissions (up to 1 ton)
  • Notice each use per substance, and a certificate of use must be attached
  • The number of online applications increased from 4 times a year to 10 times a year
Notice of Non-Phase-in substances with low-output
  • Notice the total volume of national environmental emissions (up to 10 ton) (13 times/year)
  • Notice each use per substance
  • Document to be submitted : Letter of Low-volume New Chemicals Notification, Use Confirmation Letter
Pre-confirmation of low-concern polymer
What is polymer?
  • The total weight of three or more bound molecules is at least 50% of the total, and the total weight of the same molecule is less than 50% of the total
  • Number average molecular weight (Mn) of 1,000 or more
  • Regardless of tonnage, the test proceeds according to the independent safety assessment test method [Polymer Flow-Scheme]
View details of low-concern polymer
Advantages of pre-confirmation of low-concern polymer
  • No need for pre-examination on the manufacture and import of non-Phase-in substances
  • No restrictions on manufacturing and import volume
  • Relatively short review period due to frequent receipt of application
  • Substance name not disclosed (information protection)
  • There is no obligation to [notice Annual Manufacturing and Import Volume] conducted in June of each year

Substances subject to pre-confirmation
  • Non-Phase-in chemicals with low production: subject to the substance in ‘less than 10 tpa’ and ‘not bio-accumulative’
  • Small volume of non-Phase-in chemicals: subject to the substance in ‘less than 1 tpa’
  • Intermediate: No volume limit in manufacture or import
  • Polymer of Low Concern(PLC): No volume limit in manufacture or import
Subject of low-concern polymer
In case of the following Ⅰ or Ⅱ
Ⅰ. A polymer compound that satisfies all of the following requirements
  • ① Corresponds to physicochemical stability standards
  • ② Insoluble in acid/alkali solutions.
  • ③ Insoluble in water and organic solvents.
  • ④ No metals other than Na, Mg, K, and Ca.
Ⅱ. A polymer compound that satisfies all requirements of ①, ②, and ④ of Ⅰ and ① ~ ③ below
  • ① Soluble in water and organic solvents, with a content of less than 1% of components with a molecular weight of less than 1,000 and not highly accumulating in the body
  • ② Substances that do not contain arsenic or selenium
  • ③ Substances with an Mn of 10,000 or more or all monomers that do not contain epoxy groups or sulfonic acid groups as Phase-in chemicals
