EU REACH
Registration, Evaluation, Authorization and Restriction of CHemicals
  • Integrated chemical management system stipulating registration, evaluation, authorization and restriction of chemicals in the EU
  • Due to the end of expiration date of pre-registration (May 31, 2018), the main registration is mandatory for imports or manufacturing of 1 ton or more in the EU
  • For substances without pre-registration, ‘Inquiry to ECHA’ process is required to confirm Substance Identity Profile (SIP)

Substances subject to registration
If the substance falls under the following conditions and is exported more than 1 ton per year, you can export to Europe through EU REACH registration.
  • Single
    substance
    Chemical elements and their compounds obtained in the natural state or in the manufacturing process, including additives necessary to maintain the quality and impurities generated from the process used (more than 1 ton per year)
  • Mixture
    A single substance in a mixture made up of two or more substances (more than 1 ton per year)
  • Polymer
    A single substance containing at least 2% (w/w) of the monomers or starting materials bound to the polymer and at least 1 ton per year
  • Finished product
    Finished product is exempted from registration, but substances that are intentionally emitted from the finished product (more than 1 ton per year) are subject to registration
