K-REACH
The Act on Registration and Evaluation of Chemicals (hereinafter referred to as “K-REACH”), which came into effect in 2015, stipulates matters concerning registration and notice of chemicals, examination and evaluation of hazards and risks, and designation of hazardous chemicals. It was established for the purpose of protecting public health and the environment by producing and utilizing information on chemicals.
