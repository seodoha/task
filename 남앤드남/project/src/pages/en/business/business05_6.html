China
REACH
Non-phase-in
substances control
system
China REACH
  • Applicable law
    Non-phase-in substances control system (China REACH)
  • Effective date
    October 15, 2010
  • Main obligations
    Non-phase-in substances manufacturer and importer must obtain an approval of notice from the Ministry of Environment of China before manufacturing or importing.
  • Excluded substances
    • Radioactive materials/defense industry products
    • Cigarettes, medicine, medicine for animals
    • Agricultural pesticides, cosmetics
    • Food/food additives/feed additives
Phase-in
substances list
IECSC Inventory of Existing Chemical Substance in China
  • As of 2019, about 46,000 substances listed
  • Substances not listed on Phase-in list are considered non-phase-in substances
Type of Non-Phase-in substances
  • R&D
    notification
    Ecological characteristics test sample for notice of less than 100 kg or non-phase-in substances
    • Disclosed on the website without review, not listed on the phase-in substance list.
    Submission of notice
  • Special
    Simplified
    Notification
    • 100kg -1,000kg of substances for R&D
    • Intermediates weighing less than 1,000 kg or substances for export only
    • Process R&D under 10,000 kg (up to 2 years)
    • Low-concern polymer
    No ecotoxicity data required
  • Simplified Notification
    1,000kg or less (Not registered on phase-in substance list)
    Ecotoxicity data required
  • Typical Notification
    1-10 tons, 100-1,000 tons, >1,000 tons
    Physical and chemical properties, human properties and ecotoxicity data required
