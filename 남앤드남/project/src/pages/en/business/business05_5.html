Taiwan
TCCA
Toxic Chemical
Control Act
TCSCA Toxic Chemical Substance
Control Act
  • Applicable law
    Toxic Chemical Control Act
  • Effective date
    December 11, 2014
  • Main obligations
    All non-phase-in substances manufactured or imported in Taiwan and phase-in substances of more than 0.1 ton per year must be registered to the ministry of environment of Taiwan.
  • Substances exempted from registration
    • Naturally occurring substances
    • Chemicals imported together with equipment for the purpose of pilot operation
    • Non-isolated intermediate
    • Chemicals manufactured/imported for national defense
    • Impurities/Byproducts
    • Substances under customs supervision
    • Waste
    • Chemicals controlled by other existing laws
