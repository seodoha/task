Company
Overview
NAM & NAM International Co., Ltd., founded in 1952, is a subsidiary of
NAM & NAM International Patent/Law Firm, which has led the Korean
patent industry for over half a century. We provide consulting services
for multinational companies’ local chemical business as well as methods
for responding to chemical regulations for local companies to advance
into offshore chemical business.
Thank you for visiting
Nam & Nam
International website.
Nam & Nam International, as an environmental consulting subsidiary of Nam & Nam International Patent Law Firm, which has led the Korean patent industry for over half a century, leading the way towards the future based on its high level of expertise.

Since starting the EU REACH response service for offshore companies in 2008 as a major business field, we have performed pre-registration and registration of about 2,000 substances on behalf of about 5,000 customers so far. We provide services to more than 500 companies in response to regulations on chemicals in Korea's five largest trading partners, US, Japan, Europe, China, Taiwan, and other countries.

According to local Toxic Chemical Control Act · K-REACH, about OO non-phase-in substances have been notice ed/registered, and representative registration of about OO of the 510 Phase-in substances subject to K-REACH's first joint registration was successfully performed. In addition, we have successfully carried out the registration of representatives of OO species of phase-in substance.

In addition, we provide one-stop consulting services ranging from GHS/MSDS preparation service, MSDS program, regular advice on chemicals-related laws, environmental regulations retrieval in major countries, and substance management solution development.

NAM & NAM International Co., Ltd. is always striving for renewal and renovation. As we have grown thanks to your support so far, we hope to contribute to your success and develop together.

Field of work
We provide customized services in accordance with various chemical substance regulations such as
K-REACH, K-CCA, K-BPR, Overseas Chemical Regulations, and GHS/CLP/SDS.
  • 01.
    K-REACH
    We provide related services from registration preparation to registration maintenance in accordance with K-REACH, which is essential for local chemical businesses.
  • 02.
    Chemical Control Act (CCA)
    We provide consulting on regulations related to the handling of hazardous chemicals at chemical plants.
  • 03.
    Chemical Product
    Safety Act
    We provide consulting on regulations governing the handling of biocides and household chemical products.
  • 04.
    Overseas Chemical Regulations
    Based on many years of experience and expertise, we provide consulting on chemical regulations required overseas.
  • 05.
    GHS/
    CLP/SDS
    We provide consulting on preparation, translation and revision of (M)SDS for local and overseas use according to UN GHS and EU CLP
