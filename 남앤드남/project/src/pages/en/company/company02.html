History
Nam & Nam International started its business in 2008 as an environmental consulting subsidiary of Nam & Nam International Patent Law Firm. We will tell you about the path we have taken to develop into the company we are today through our rich experience.
History
2008 - Present
  • 2022 ~ Present
    Start of Tier Ⅱ service for phase-in substances
    Eurasia REACH, India REACH service (planned)
  • 2021
    (2019~) K-REACH Phase-in substances over 1,000 tons (Tier I) OO case registration completed
    UK REACH service started
  • 2019
    K-BPR(Biocides & Household chemicals) service started
    Turkey REACH service started
  • 2018
    Representative registration of about OO types of ‘510 kinds of Phase-in substances subject to K-REACH registration’ completed.
    2010-2018 EU REACH, registration of multiple chemicals completed.
  • 2015
    K-REACH Small and Medium Enterprise support project (Ministry of Environment)
    K-REACH Joint Registration Consortium Advisor for Korea Petrochemical Association
    2015.7 K-REACH representative registration service started
  • 2014
    Establishment of K-REACH alternative substance DB (Ministry of Trade, Industry and Energy)
    K-REACH response IT system establishment (Ministry of Trade, Industry and Energy)
    K-REACH joint registration pilot project (Ministry of Environment)
    China REACH service started
    Japan CSCL service started
    Taiwan REACH service started
  • 2013
    Development and application of guidelines related to K-REACH (Ministry of Environment)
  • 2012
    A study on industrial support plan according to the introduction of K-REACH (Korea Institute of Industrial Technology)
  • 2011
    A study on the introduction of hazardous substances registration and notice system for products (Ministry of Knowledge Economy)
    MSDS authoring program(software) service started
  • 2010
    Registered as an environmental consulting company by the Ministry of Environment
    Joined as a special member of Korea Environmental Consulting Association
  • 2008
    Established Nam & Nam Europe GmbH.
    Established NAM & NAM International Co., Ltd.
