Social
contribution
Nam & Nam International is conducting differentiated social contribution
activities by systematically maintaining and developing social
contribution activities. Representative activities include a bazaar event
hosted by Nam & Nam International consultants, and through the event,
we support welfare and donation groups at home and abroad.
NAM & NAM International is contributing to the
social communities by means of various support
activities for the local community.
We want to contribute not only for clients’ success but also for the development of local community and making a better world. Consultants of NAM & NAM International Co., Ltd. regularly hold events such as year-end bazaars for people and groups in need, and all members cooperate to support welfare and donation institutions at home and abroad.
